Who are We?

ChopNowNow(CNN) is one of the leading brands in the Quick Service Restaurant Industry in Nigeria. CNN was incorporated in 1994 as a private company and strives to operate with integrity and trust to create value for our customers and staff.

We offer a wide variety of quality and affordable meals in a relaxed ambience. Our signature dishes are our Yam Pottage, Jamboree Rice and Bullah rice.
